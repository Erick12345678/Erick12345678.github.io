How to run this on your iOS device.

1. Copy the zip file to any mac, linux, or other unix based system. Download Zip

2. Double click the zip file to decompress it. You will get a folder named FIU_AR_QUICK_LOOK

3. Open a terminal window.

4. Type cd, then drag the folder into the terminal, and press enter.

5. Type ipconfig getifaddr en0 into the terminal if you are on a mac, or hostname -i if on linux/unix and press enter. You will get the local IP address of your computer. You will get something like 10.0.0.8, your's will likely be different, so take note of it.

6. Type python -m SimpleHTTPServer into the terminal, this will start the server on your local network.

7. On your iPhone open Safari, and type in http:// followed by the address obtained in step 5. You will enter something like http://10.0.0.8

8. Now tap on any link, and you are now ready to enjoy AR quick look on your device.

AR Quick look requires an iOS device with an A9 chip or later running iOS 12 or later.
