Map of FIU’s main campus for AR(augmented reality) on iOS devices

Run this on your iPhone today!


The time has come to see a map of FIU in AR, and you will be amazed!


Simply open the usdz file and tap on AR to test on your device.

Note: It is best to test in a brightly lit area with a flat floor.


Tap anywhere on your screen to place the model,  pinch to zoom in or out, and walk around the model to get the full AR experience. In a few years we may even have AR glasses, so make sure to save this model for as long as possible.


WARNING: 

Do NOT do this in the middle of the street!

As awesome as this technology is, no one wants to be watching the news and see that an FIU student was hit by a car because they were trying out AR on their iPhone in the middle of the street. If anyone does that, they deserve a Darwin Award.


If it does not work make sure your device is up to date by going to Settings > General > Software Update, and installing any available updates. Also check the device list, and make sure your device is on the list.


If the model keeps moving around by itself, glitches out, or if it won’t get placed on the ground, try moving to a brighter area, and trying again.


Please send, and distribute this to as many FIU students with iPhones, and/or iPads as possible. Also, make sure to copy and paste all these words as well. We need to make sure everyone gets this information, as it should help answer any questions they may have, and troubleshoot any issues they may experience.


Now here’s an interesting fact about maps in this area. Here in Miami-Dade county we have a consistent county-wide street numbering system going from SW 424th St near Florida City to NE 215th St near Aventura. However up in Broward this is not the case. For example if you start in Margate, and start heading south from there you will hit an area where you have SW 20th St located only a couple blocks from NW 60th St. This can be very confusing to those who are unfamiliar with that area. There are some similar cases elsewhere in broward.


You can see this anomaly by looking at the address below on any map or GPS app. 

4001 NW 62nd St

North Lauderdale, FL  33319

United States
